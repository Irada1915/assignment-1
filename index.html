Tracking my screen time

I used the Screen Time function on my phone to track how much time I spend on different apps and websites each week. This data helps me understand my digital habits and whether I need to make adjustments to my screen time.

Screen time by days of the week

Peak device usage occurs on weekends, especially Saturday, while Tuesday shows minimal activity. This suggests more active device use during free time.

Screen time distribution by categories

The majority of screen time is spent on social networks, which may indicate active communication and content consumption. Entertainment takes the second-largest share, while productive tasks make up a smaller portion.

Hourly Activity on January 21

There are three peak periods of device usage (morning, noon, and evening), with activity decreasing between them. This may indicate regular patterns of device checking at specific times of the day.

Overall, this data highlights the importance of mindful screen time management, as excessive use of social media and entertainment apps can impact productivity, sleep, and overall well-being. It may be beneficial for me to reevaluate phone usage habits by implementing limits or adopting a more balanced approach.
